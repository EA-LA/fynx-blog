TL;DR

Guardian is your risk and emotion firewall: it detects behavior drift early and protects you from the mistakes that blow accounts.

What it tracks

  • Risk behavior consistency (over-risking, rule breaks)
  • Streak patterns (wins/losses affecting decisions)
  • Revenge trading signals (speed + size + frequency shifts)

How to use it (simple workflow)

  • After each session: log the trade + tag the reason
  • Weekly: review “what triggered mistakes”
  • Before a new trade: confirm your rules are still true
